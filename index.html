TAKEAWAYS FROM THE DATA

What does the data shown above show about the relationship between cat and dog populations and ownership?

Pet Cat vs Dog Population

What's up with pet cats and dogs over the past years?

From the bar charts, we can see that the population of pet cats have increased on average over time, while the population of pet dogs have decreased. The decrease in pet dog population is such that the total population of pet cats is higher than total population of pet dogs.

Pet Cat vs Dog Ownership

What about pet ownership?

From the line charts, we can see that the number of pet dog owners has decreased over time, while the number of pet cat owners has roughly managed to maintain over time. However, as a whole, the number of pet dog owners is still higher than the number of pet cat owners across all years.

Pet Cat Population vs Ownership

Is there any correlation between pet cat population and ownership?

Comparing between the 2 graphs, pet cat population increased on average, while ownership maintained. This may be an indicator that pet cat owners may own more than 1 pet cat.

Pet Dog Population vs Ownership

What about for pet dogs?

Both pet dog population and pet down owners are decreasing, which indicate that less pet owners are getting pet dogs. However, it is important to note that while pet dog population is lower than pet cat population, the number of pet dog owners are higher than pet cat owners. This is further indication that pet cat owners may own more than 1 pet cat, while also being indicative that pet dog owners only own 1 dog.

Recent Global Events and Pets

It is important to note that global and local events may have affected the acquisation of pets in Japan, causing the trends that we saw above.

For instance, the Covid-19 pandemic in 2020 caused a reduction in human interaction in Japan. According to news reports, pet ownership increased during Covid-19 due to an increase in the need for companionship. This can be noted from the increase in pet cat population, as well as pet cat ownership, from 2020 to 2021.

On the other hand, the Russia-Ukraine war that broke out in early 2022 has caused pet food prices to increase, which may be a factor in causing pet populations to decrease in 2022 as pets are less affordable.

CONCLUSION

What can we conlude about pet cats and dogs in Japan?

Popularity of cats and dogs

As pets

Cats and Dogs are the most popular pets in Japan, surpassing other types of pets by large percentages. However, it is still important to note that majority (72.8%) of Japanese do not own pets.

Population and Ownership

The relationship

While pet cat population is higher than pet dog population, the ownership of pet dogs is higher than that of pet cats, indicating that pet cat owners typically own more than 1 cat.

Reasons for keeping pet cats

Linking to population and ownership

The top reason for keeping pet cats is by adopting strays, making up 30.5% of reasons. This is followed by because the previous pet passed away, and because a friend asked the owner to take care of the cat. This shows that adopting stray cats is a popular way of getting a pet cat, and may explain the high amount of pet cats despite lower number of pet owners. We also observe sentimental value of having a pet being a factor, with owners replacing pet cats that passed away.

Reasons for keeping pet dogs

Constrasting with that for cats

The top reason for keeping dogs is because the previous pet passed away (at 26%), followed by seeing it in a pet shop and desiring it. This shows that the main reason owners get pet dogs is for sentimental value, to replace their previous pets. Other than that, owners also get pet dogs because of the popularity of dogs as pets, hence getting them from pet stores.
