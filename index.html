PETS IN JAPAN

RESEARCH QUESTION:

Ever wondered how pet ownership looks like in Japan? What are the most common pets kept in Japan?

How have the ownership of common pets changed over time, and why have they changed?

In a survey done in October 2022, the total number of respondents was 50,000, and 72.80% of them do not own pets

ABOUT PET CATS AND DOGS IN JAPAN

According to respondents in 2022, even though dogs were the most desired pet species among Japanese people, cats are closing in on the popularity of canines.

From this data, some questions can be asked regarding the ownership of pet cats and dogs:

1. What are the pet cat and dog ownership trends in the past few years?

2. What are the main reasons why people decide to keep cats and dogs as pets?

3. Did the Covid-19 pandemic in 2019 affect cat and dog ownership trends?
